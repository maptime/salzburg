After some mapathons and social events, this time we had a more technical topic again. We were very happy that [Luuk van der Meer](https://twitter.com/LuukvanderMeer), researcher at [Z_GIS](http://zgis.at/), kindly gave a short introduction to R for spatial analysis. Afterwards we had a hands-on workshop with some exercises Luuk had prepared.
